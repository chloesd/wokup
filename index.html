Why is it better to eat local and seasonal?

  • To take advantage of nutritional benefits:
    Each season responds to a need of the human body. In summer, with the heat, our body expends fewer calories but requires more water: all the fruits and vegetables of the season are bursting with it: melons, tomatoes, courgettes, watermelons, etc.

  • To favour local fruits and vegetables:
    Imported fruit and vegetables mature during transport!

  • To have a less polluting impact:
    The closer the fruits and vegetables have been produced to your home, the better it will be for limiting your carbon impact and the less likely they will have been grown in greenhouses.

  • To support local farming:
    Eating local means finding fruits and vegetables that have taste, nutritional richness and vitamins while encouraging the emergence of local producers.
